Personal Information

I'm Rainzell Andrei Espaldon, an 18-year-old BSIT student from Gulod, Cabuyao, Laguna. I have a strong passion for technology and enjoy exploring how digital tools can be used to solve problems and improve everyday life. As someone who is naturally curious, I am always eager to learn new concepts, develop my skills, and challenge myself through both academic and personal projects.

Outside of my studies, I enjoy playing video games, photography, videography, and content editing. These hobbies have helped me develop creativity, attention to detail, and a deeper appreciation for multimedia. Whether I'm capturing moments through a camera lens or editing videos and photos, I enjoy bringing ideas to life and expressing creativity through different forms of digital media.

Information Technology has always been my first choice because of my genuine interest in computers, software, and emerging technologies. My passion for technology motivates me to continuously learn and grow in this field. In the future, I aspire to become a Full Stack Developer, where I can design, build, and maintain innovative applications and systems. My goal is to code, create, and innovate solutions that make a meaningful impact while contributing to the ever-evolving world of technology.

Educational Background

I'm currently a 2nd year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Pamantasan ng Cabuyao (University of Cabuyao). Throughout my studies, I've gained knowledge in programming, database management, and human-computer interaction. Beyond the classroom, I enjoy exploring web development on my own, building projects with HTML and CSS to strengthen my skills and turn what I learn into real, working applications. My passion for technology motivates me to keep learning and improving as I work toward becoming a skilled IT professional.
